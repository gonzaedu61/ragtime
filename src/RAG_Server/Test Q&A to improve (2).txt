(ragtime) PS C:\dev\ragtime> rag "What can Stamm Lohn be refferring to" pepe.json
“Stamm Lohn” is the central payroll master‐data record for each employee.  In it you maintain:

• Personal number (must match the external payroll system)  
• Employee name  
• “Kst‐Lohnausgleich” (the default cost center for payroll equalization)  
• Verrechnungssatz (billing or cost rate used in the “Lohnausgleich” program)  
• Flag whether daily time sheets (Tageszettel) may be recorded  
• Assigned time‐accounts for weekly/monthly payroll closings  
• Parameters for exporting the payroll data (e.g. date‐range, personnel numbers, cost centers) to external systems (CSS, Datev, Carat, SAP, etc.)
(ragtime) PS C:\dev\ragtime> rag "What can Stammlohn be refferring to" pepe.json 
Stammlohn in this context is not a special calculation rule at all but simply the “wage master data” — i.e. the employee’s basic payroll‐record (the Stammdaten for Lohn) maintained in the system.  Concretely it is defined in the “stamm_lohn” workshop and typically contains

• Personal number  
• Employee name  
• Default cost centre (Kst-Lohnausgleich)  
• Billing rate (Verrechnungssatz)  
• Flag whether time‐sheets may be recorded (Tageszettelerfassung)  

All of your Lohn-Rules and the automatic recalculation of wage types (Lohnarten) refer back to this master wage record.
