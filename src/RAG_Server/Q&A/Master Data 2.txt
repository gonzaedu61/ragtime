Here is a high-level overview of the main master-data topic areas in ALMENDRO, as reflected by the available PDFs and descriptions:

• “Stammdaten Sonstige” (stamm_sonst.pdf)  
  – Miscellaneous master-data settings and flags that don’t fall into other categories

• “Stammdaten Adressen” (stamm_adr.pdf)  
  – Address administration (customers, suppliers, contacts, etc.)

• Nummernbereiche (© ALMENDRO GmbH – 2026 52 Kz)  
  – Configuration of document- and number-ranges used system-wide

• “Stammdaten Kalkulation” (stamm_kalk.pdf)  
  – Cost-center definitions  
  – Operation (“Arbeitsgang”) data  
  – Service-catalogue entries  
  – Special calculation functions enabled by master-data flags

• “Stammdaten Material” (stamm_mat.pdf)
  – Material master (raw materials, semi-finished goods)
  – Article master
  – Tool definitions

• Seller (ALMENDRO-mandant) payment-receipt data
  – Bank and payment-processing parameters maintained in the seller’s master data

• Betriebsdatenerfassung (leist_bde.pdf)
  – Shop-floor and time-capture configuration

• Text-type configuration
  – Definition and assignment of free-text fields in various masters (see Workshop “Basis”)

• Positionskalkulation master data
  – Data structures used in per-item costing (see Workshop “Positionskalkulation”)

• Reference-variable (“Bezugsgröße”) definitions
  – Quantities, paper-weight, tiered runs, interpolation keys, etc., which must be set up before being used in calculation or costing

Each of these topic areas is covered in one or more “stamm_*.pdf” documents or associated workshops, and together they form the backbone of ALMENDRO’s configurable master-data framework.
